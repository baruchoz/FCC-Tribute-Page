Wendy Carlos

An innovator of electronic music

Wendy Carlos spending time in her studio with her cat. She was an innovator for electronic music and a master of synthesizers.

A Timeline of Wendy Carlos' Life

  • 1939 - Born in Pawtucket, Rhode Island
  • 1962 - Moved to New York City to study music composition at Columbia University.
  • 1968 - Carlos came to prominence with Switched-On Bach, an album of music by Johann Sebastian Bach performed on a Moog synthesizer
  • 1979 - Carlos raised public awareness of transgender issues by disclosing she had been living as a woman since at least 1968, and in 1972 had undergone sex reassignment surgery.
